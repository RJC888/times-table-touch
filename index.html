The Science Behind It

Times Table Touch uses kinesthetic learning and cross-brain coordination to help students memorize multiplication facts faster and more effectively.

  • Left Brain & Right Hand: Students use their RIGHT hand to touch the LEFT column
  • Right Brain & Left Hand: Students use their LEFT hand to touch the RIGHT column
  • Multi-Sensory Learning: Touch + Visual + Auditory = Better Retention

Classroom Implementation

  • Groups of 3-4 students work best
  • Each student gets 2-3 turns (about 5 minutes total)
  • Students recite each number OUT LOUD as they touch it
  • Start from the bottom and work upward
  • Complete the "Keep Going" challenges for extra practice
